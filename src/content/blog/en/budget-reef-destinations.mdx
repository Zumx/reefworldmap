This is our working list for budget reef destinations — world-class reefs without the resort price tag. The picks come from our own travel logs, conversations with operators, and reader correspondence. Each entry links through to its site on the [interactive map](/map) where you can check season, access, and operator listings.

## 1. Utila

Strong for first-timers and for travellers wanting a soft introduction to the genre.

## 2. Dahab

An established option that consistently delivers on its reputation.

## 3. Koh Tao

Slightly off the main tourist track; rewards a bit of research.

## 4. Bunaken

Best in a specific window — check the [interactive map](/map) for current conditions.

## 5. Caye Caulker

A standout that justifies the trip on its own.

## 6. Apo Island

Pairs well with a nearby site for a two-week itinerary.

## 7. Coron

Good infrastructure and a healthy local conservation culture.

## 8. Marsa Alam

Cooler-water counterpart with its own character.
